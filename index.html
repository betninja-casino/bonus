Bonus Terms & Wagering Requirements

The wagering requirement specifies how many times you must wager bonus funds before withdrawing winnings.

Example: 30× wagering on a €50 bonus means €1,500 must be wagered on eligible games before withdrawal.
  • Eligible games — slots usually count 100%; table games and live casino may contribute less.
  • Maximum bet — most bonuses cap the stake during wagering.
  • Time limits — bonuses often expire if wagering isn't completed in time.
  • Withdrawal restrictions — bonus funds can't be withdrawn; only winnings after meeting wagering.
